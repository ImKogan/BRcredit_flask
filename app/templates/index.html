About BRcredit

BRcredit was conceived as a solution to the exorbitant APR credit card rates in Brazil. In our analysis, the abnormally high rates facing the Brazilian consumer stem from two factors.

  • The near monopoly of the top five (Itau, Banco do Brasil, Bradesco, Santander, Caixa) institutions in the credit market
  • The lack of complete and reliable information that would allow lenders to distinguish between high and medium risk applicants

While the first factor can be theoretically addressed by new entrants into the market, the second factor requires a more comprehensive solution. Our analysis views the consumer credit market in 3 tiers:

  • Top tier of consumers (< 10%) who enjoy relatively low APR rates of approximately 100%
  • A middle tier (~30%) that is subject to the average rates that have been as high as 400%
  • Finally, a lower tier that generally has limited or no access to established credit sources

While the brazilian market has seen some new entrants (Goldman Sachs report covered here in the NYT), the market is large and fragmented enough to acommodate many more entrants.

Enter BRcredit

BRcredit is a multi-staged approach that takes advantage of the market opportunity (relative monopoly and high rates) while tackling the barrier of low quality information.

Stage 1 - Offer lower rates to medium or high risk applicants - for whom data is unreliable or incomplete - provided a guarantor with low risk credentials. Although some higher risk individuals would be unable to provide such a guarantor, many would. During this stage BRcredit will be able to study the credit habits of many consumers who are either discouraged from the current market by prohibitive rates, donâ€™t carry a balance, or default on their loans.

BRcredit: The appplication - how it works

This application is a prototype. To get a sense of how to use it:
  • Create two accounts. One will be the "guarantor" account, the second, the 'borrpwer" account. An account will require a valid email address
  • In the "borrower" account follow instructions in the 'Connect Guarantor' link
  • In the "guarantor" account accept the request to connect
  • The "borrower" can fill and submit a loan application
  • The "guarantor" can submit his information for the loan application.
  • As this is a prototype - a loan application is set to be automatically 'approved'. Both borrower and guarantor can folllow the link 'Manage Loans' to view and make payments.

Some aspects of the process may appear peculiar - such as interest rates denominated in monthly terms - fixed installment plans and so on. These are based on the specifics of the Brazilian market. The latter especially so - as the option to 'parcelar' - to pay in installments is practically ubiquitous throughout the consumer economy. Any purchase - from vacations to flights, mattresses to houswares can be paid in installments. These are financed by the vendor or a third party. This feature of Brazilian consumer culture is in our opinion a strong positive predictor for the viability of our model.
